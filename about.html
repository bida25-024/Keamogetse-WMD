A Love Story Brewed Over Time

Kelly's Cafe was born from a simple dream: to create a space where coffee isn't just a drink—it's an experience. Founded in 2020, we set out to bring specialty coffee to Gaborone with a focus on quality, sustainability, and community.

Our beans are ethically sourced from small-scale farmers around the world, roasted locally to perfection, and brewed with precision by our skilled baristas. Every cup tells a story of dedication and passion.

Today, Kelly's Cafe has become a beloved gathering spot for coffee lovers, remote workers, and friends catching up over lattes. We're honored to be part of your daily ritual.
