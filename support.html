Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open every day of the week from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On public holidays, our hours may vary, so please check our social media for updates.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver within a 5km radius of our location. Delivery fee is ₱150. Orders over ₱500 get free delivery.

Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?

Absolutely! We partner directly with small-scale farmers who use sustainable practices. All our beans are fair trade certified.

Do you offer non-dairy milk options?

Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk at no extra charge. Just let us know your preference when ordering.

Can I order a custom cake?

Yes! We take custom cake orders for birthdays, weddings, and other celebrations. Please allow 3-5 days notice. Contact us directly for pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile money. All payments are secure and processed through our trusted partners.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 2 hours before pickup/delivery are free. For custom orders, please cancel at least 24 hours in advance.
